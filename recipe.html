Condensed Milk Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk
  • 3 ovos
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

Preparation

Syrup

  1. Melt the sugar in the pan until it turns brown
  2. Add water to the melted sugar and let it thicken
  3. Place in a round baking dish

Pudding

  1. Beat the eggs well in the blender
  2. Add condensed milk and milk, and beat again
  3. Pour the pudding dough into the same mold in which the syrup was placed, with the dough remaining on top of the syrup
  4. Bake in an oven at medium temperature for 45 minutes, with the round baking dish inside a larger one filled with water
